Histoy of Octane

Octane's real name is Octavio Silva. He hails from the planet Psamathe, which is also the homeworld of another legend, Lifeline. Before becoming a Legend, Octane was a thrill-seeker and daredevil who became bored with his mundane life and sought the adrenaline rush of the Apex Games.

Traits of Octane

Octane is portrayed as energetic, impulsive, and somewhat reckless. He often makes quips and jokes during matches, embodying a carefree and adventurous spirit. He is depicted as having a mechanical leg, which he lost during one of his daredevil stunts, further highlighting his risk-taking nature.
